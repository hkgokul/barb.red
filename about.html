About

BARB Studio is the visualization and digital production wing of the BARB ecosystem — supporting architects and designers with high‑fidelity 3D imagery, animations, and spatial storytelling.

We deliver white‑label visualization for studios worldwide, blending technical precision with design intent.

  • Scope: Exterior & interior stills, competition visuals, concept renders, 3D animation.
  • Turnaround: 48–72 hours for a hero still.
  • Pipelines: SketchUp / Revit / 3ds Max + Corona/V‑Ray.
  • Compliance: NDA‑friendly, multi‑timezone delivery (IST / GST).
