Business Model

Brand & Entity Architecture

Umbrella brand: BARB (master brand & design system)

Non-profit (public-facing)

BARB Foundation (Section 8 company, India)
Programs: BARB Care (health/mental health), BARB Skills (CAD training), BARB Civic (ramps, sanitation, shade, water).
Funding: Indian CSR & grants, domestic donations (80G later).
Note: Avoid foreign donations until FCRA secured.

For-profit engines (arms-length, same brand family)

  • BARB Stays Pvt Ltd — rentals & hospitality.
  • BARB Kitchen LLP — food services near campuses/industrial belts.
  • BARB Studio Pvt Ltd (India) + BARB Studio FZ-LLC (UAE) — 2D/3D outsourcing.
  • Property SPVs (LLP per site) — hold each rental asset separately.

Program Design (Foundation)

BARB Care

Clinics, rural mental-health sites, and campus hubs with stigma-reducing design.

BARB Skills

3-month bootcamps in CAD & visualization, placement pipeline into Studio and engines.

BARB Civic

Kits for ramps, sanitation, shade, water — drawings, CSI specs, BOQ, QA checklist.

Revenue Engines

BARB Stays

Luxury rentals near Tier-2/3 city edges and daily rentals near campuses.

BARB Kitchen

Campus-adjacent food services, subscription tiffin, cloud kitchen model.

BARB Studio

2D/3D outsourcing, UAE entity invoices, India entity executes.

The Flywheel

  1. Skills → Talent: youth trained in Skills join engines.
  2. Profit → Impact: engines contribute % to Foundation.
  3. Impact → Brand: CSR projects boost trust & MOUs.
  4. Engines → Pipeline: Stays/Kitchen reinforce BARB presence.

Numbers (Illustrative)

  • Campus Stay (40 beds, IIT KGP) → ₹59L EBITDA; 10% = ₹5.9L donation.
  • Luxury Stay (6 keys, TVM) → ₹63L EBITDA; 10% = ₹6.3L donation.
  • BARB Studio (8 drafters) → ₹31.5L EBITDA; 10% = ₹3.1L donation.

Clinic upgrades: ₹18–22L; School sanitation kits: ₹9–15L; Access kits: ₹6–12L.

12-Month Roadmap

From registration & pilots (Day 0–90) → scaling (Month 4–9) → consolidation (Month 10–12). Deliver 4–6 sites and generate ₹80L–1.2Cr engine revenue in Year 1.

Governance

Engines donate voluntarily (min % EBITDA); Foundation remains domestic until FCRA; clear KPIs across Care, Skills, Civic, and engines.

Risks & Mitigations

Compliance, utilization risk, quality drift, talent churn, and reputation — mitigated by modular capex, published kits, continuous training, and audits.
